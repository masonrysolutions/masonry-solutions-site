Gallery

To add 200+ photos (antivirus-safe, no scripts):

  1. Copy your photos into the gallery-images folder.
  2. Rename them in order (e.g. 001.jpg, 002.jpg, 003.jpg).
  3. Open gallery.html in Notepad and copy/paste the tile line for each photo.
Example tile line to copy:
<div class="tile"><img src="gallery-images/001.jpg" alt="Project photo"></div>
